## Marinade

**Marinade** is the leading liquid staking protocol on Solana. It allows users to stake their SOL tokens to earn staking rewards while receiving mSOL (Marinade staked SOL) tokens in return. These mSOL tokens can be used across DeFi protocols, so you don't have to choose between earning staking rewards and participating in DeFi.

### How It Works

When you deposit SOL into Marinade, you receive mSOL tokens that represent your staked SOL plus accumulated rewards. The value of mSOL relative to SOL increases over time as staking rewards accrue. You can use mSOL in lending protocols, liquidity pools, or as collateral, all while your underlying SOL continues earning staking rewards.

### Why It Matters

Traditional staking locks your tokens, making them unavailable for other uses. Marinade solves this by giving you a liquid representation of your staked assets. This concept, known as liquid staking, has become one of the most important DeFi primitives. Marinade also helps decentralize the network by distributing stake across hundreds of validators rather than concentrating it with a few large ones.
