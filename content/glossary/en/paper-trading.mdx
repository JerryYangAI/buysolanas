## Paper Trading

**Paper trading** is the practice of simulating trades with virtual money to test strategies and learn market mechanics without risking real capital. It provides a risk-free environment for beginners and a testing ground for experienced traders developing new strategies.

### How It Works

Paper trading platforms simulate real market conditions using live price data, but trades are executed with virtual funds. Traders can practice buying, selling, setting stop-losses, and managing positions exactly as they would with real money. Performance is tracked to evaluate strategy effectiveness before committing real capital.

### Why It Matters

Paper trading is invaluable for learning without financial risk. New crypto traders can familiarize themselves with DEX interfaces, understand order types, and experience market volatility before investing real money. Experienced traders use paper trading to backtest new strategies without capital exposure.
