## Whitelist

A **whitelist** (also called allowlist) is a curated list of wallet addresses that are given early or guaranteed access to mint NFTs before they become available to the general public. Whitelisted wallets often get priority pricing or guaranteed allocation.

### How It Works

NFT projects distribute whitelist spots through community engagement — completing tasks, winning contests, being active in Discord, or holding specific tokens. During the mint, whitelisted addresses can mint before the public sale starts, often at a lower price or with guaranteed availability. The smart contract verifies that the minting wallet is on the list.

### Why It Matters

Whitelists reward early supporters and community members, creating a fair distribution mechanism for high-demand NFT launches. They help prevent bot attacks during minting and give genuine community members a better chance of acquiring NFTs at launch price rather than paying secondary market premiums.
