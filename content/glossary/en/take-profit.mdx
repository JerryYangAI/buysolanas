## Take-Profit

A **take-profit** order automatically sells an asset when it reaches a predetermined profit target. It locks in gains by executing a sale at a favorable price without requiring the trader to manually monitor the market.

### How It Works

Traders set a take-profit price above their entry price. When the market reaches this level, the order triggers and the position is sold. This can be implemented as a limit sell order on Solana DEXs. Some traders use multiple take-profit levels to scale out of positions gradually, securing partial profits along the way.

### Why It Matters

Take-profit orders enforce trading discipline by removing emotion from the sell decision. Without them, traders often fall prey to greed, holding positions too long and watching profits evaporate. Combined with stop-losses, take-profit orders create a complete risk-reward framework for every trade.
