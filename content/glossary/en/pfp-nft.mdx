## PFP NFT

A **PFP NFT** (profile picture NFT) is a type of non-fungible token specifically designed to be used as a social media avatar or profile picture. PFP collections typically consist of thousands of algorithmically generated characters with varying traits and rarity levels.

### How It Works

PFP collections use generative art techniques to create thousands of unique images from layered traits like backgrounds, clothing, accessories, and expressions. Each combination produces a unique NFT with different rarity. Owners use these as their online identity across social platforms, signaling community membership.

### Why It Matters

PFP NFTs transformed NFTs from collectibles into digital identity. Owning a popular PFP signals community membership, grants access to exclusive events and groups, and serves as a form of social currency in Web3. Collections like Mad Lads on Solana have built strong communities around shared identity.
