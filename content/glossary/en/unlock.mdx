## Unlock

A **token unlock** is a scheduled event where previously locked tokens become available for their holders to sell, transfer, or use. Unlocks increase the circulating supply and can impact token price if recipients choose to sell.

### How It Works

Token unlocks follow the vesting schedule established at the project's inception. They can be cliff unlocks (large one-time releases), linear unlocks (continuous small releases), or periodic unlocks (monthly/quarterly batches). Token unlock trackers help investors monitor upcoming unlock events across the crypto ecosystem.

### Why It Matters

Large token unlocks are among the most significant supply-side events for a cryptocurrency. When millions of dollars worth of tokens become available to early investors or team members who may want to realize profits, the resulting sell pressure can drive prices down. Savvy investors track unlock schedules to anticipate these events.
