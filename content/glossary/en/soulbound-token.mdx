## Soulbound Token

A **soulbound token** (SBT) is a non-transferable NFT that is permanently bound to a specific wallet address. Once received, it cannot be sold, traded, or transferred, making it ideal for representing credentials, achievements, and identity attributes.

### How It Works

Soulbound tokens use the same underlying NFT technology but with transfer restrictions enforced by the smart contract. On Solana, Token Extensions supports non-transferable tokens natively. SBTs can represent university degrees, professional certifications, event attendance, reputation scores, or community membership.

### Why It Matters

SBTs enable on-chain identity and reputation systems. Unlike transferable tokens, they truly represent the holder's achievements and attributes. This opens up use cases like uncollateralized lending based on reputation, verifiable credentials, and governance systems weighted by contribution rather than wealth.
