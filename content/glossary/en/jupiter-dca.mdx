## Jupiter DCA

**Jupiter DCA** is a dollar-cost averaging tool built into the Jupiter aggregator on Solana. It allows users to automatically split a large token purchase or sale into smaller, regularly scheduled trades over a specified time period.

### How It Works

Users set the total amount they want to trade, the token pair, the frequency of trades, and the duration. Jupiter then automatically executes smaller trades at each interval, using its aggregation engine to find the best prices across Solana DEXs. This smooths out the entry or exit price over time.

### Why It Matters

DCA is a proven investment strategy that reduces the impact of volatility. Jupiter DCA makes this strategy accessible directly on-chain without needing a centralized exchange. It is particularly useful for accumulating tokens during volatile markets or gradually exiting large positions without causing significant price impact.
