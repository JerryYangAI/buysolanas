## NFT (Non-Fungible Token)

An **NFT (Non-Fungible Token)** is a unique digital asset stored on the blockchain that proves ownership of a specific item — such as digital art, music, virtual land, or collectibles. Unlike regular tokens (which are fungible, meaning each one is identical), every NFT is one-of-a-kind and cannot be exchanged equally for another.

### How It Works

When a creator makes an NFT, they "mint" it on the blockchain, creating a permanent record of its existence and ownership. The NFT itself is a token that contains metadata pointing to the associated content (image, video, etc.) and tracks its ownership history. On Solana, NFTs follow the Metaplex standard and can be created and traded with extremely low fees compared to other blockchains.

### Why It Matters

NFTs have opened up new possibilities for digital ownership, enabling creators to sell their work directly to collectors while earning royalties on secondary sales. On Solana, the low minting costs (often less than $0.01) have made NFTs accessible to creators of all sizes. Popular Solana NFT marketplaces include Magic Eden and Tensor.
