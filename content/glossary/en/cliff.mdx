## Cliff

A **cliff** is the initial waiting period in a token vesting schedule during which no tokens are released to the recipient. After the cliff period ends, a significant portion of tokens may unlock all at once, followed by gradual releases.

### How It Works

In a typical "4-year vest with 1-year cliff," no tokens are accessible for the first 12 months. At the cliff date, 25% of the total allocation unlocks immediately. After that, the remaining tokens vest monthly or quarterly over the remaining 3 years. If the recipient leaves the project before the cliff, they receive no tokens.

### Why It Matters

Cliffs protect projects from short-term participants who might dump tokens quickly. For investors, upcoming cliff dates for large allocations (team, investors) are critical events to monitor. A major cliff expiration can create sudden selling pressure that impacts token price.
