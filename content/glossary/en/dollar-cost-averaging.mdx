## Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)

**Dollar-cost averaging** (DCA) is an investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals regardless of the asset's price. This approach reduces the impact of volatility by averaging your purchase price over time.

### How It Works

Instead of investing a lump sum at one point, you split it into equal portions and invest periodically. For example, investing $100 in SOL every week for a year. When prices are low, you buy more tokens; when prices are high, you buy fewer. Over time, this tends to result in a lower average cost per token compared to trying to time the market.

### Why It Matters

DCA removes the emotional stress of deciding when to buy and eliminates the risk of investing everything at a market peak. It is one of the most recommended strategies for long-term crypto investors. On Solana, Jupiter DCA automates this strategy directly on-chain.
