## Consensus Mechanism

A **consensus mechanism** is the protocol that allows all participants in a decentralized blockchain network to agree on which transactions are valid and what the current state of the ledger is, without needing a central authority.

### How It Works

Different blockchains use different consensus mechanisms. Proof of Work (PoW) requires miners to solve computational puzzles. Proof of Stake (PoS) selects validators based on their staked tokens. Solana combines Proof of Stake with Proof of History (PoH) for time-ordering, enabling extremely fast consensus.

### Why It Matters

The consensus mechanism determines a blockchain's security, speed, energy usage, and decentralization properties. It is the core design choice that shapes everything about a blockchain's performance and trade-offs. Solana's innovative consensus approach enables its high throughput and low fees.
