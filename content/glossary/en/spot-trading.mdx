## Spot Trading

**Spot trading** is the most basic form of trading where assets are bought and sold for immediate settlement at the current market price. When you buy SOL on a DEX or exchange, you are spot trading — you pay now and receive the tokens immediately.

### How It Works

In spot trading, the buyer pays the current market price and receives the asset instantly (or within the block confirmation time on-chain). There is no leverage, no expiration date, and no derivative contracts involved. You own the actual tokens and can transfer, stake, or use them in DeFi immediately after purchase.

### Why It Matters

Spot trading is the foundation of all crypto markets. It is the simplest and safest way to buy and sell crypto — you always know exactly what you own and there is no risk of liquidation. For most users, spot trading on platforms like Jupiter or Raydium is the primary way they interact with the Solana ecosystem.
