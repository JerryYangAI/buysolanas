## Jupiter

**Jupiter** is the most popular decentralized exchange (DEX) aggregator on Solana. Rather than being a single exchange, Jupiter scans across multiple DEXs and liquidity pools to find users the best possible price for their token swaps, all in a single transaction.

### How It Works

When you want to swap one token for another, Jupiter checks prices across dozens of Solana DEXs (like Raydium, Orca, and others) and routes your trade through the path that gives you the most tokens for your money. It might even split your trade across multiple routes to minimize slippage and get a better overall rate.

### Why It Matters

Jupiter has become the default trading interface for the Solana ecosystem, handling billions of dollars in trading volume. Its aggregation service saves users money by always finding the best rate, and its simple interface makes token swapping accessible even for beginners. Jupiter also offers limit orders, dollar-cost averaging, and perpetual trading features.
