## Orca

**Orca** is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on Solana, recognized for its intuitive user interface and innovative concentrated liquidity model called Whirlpools. It is one of the most popular trading venues in the Solana ecosystem.

### How It Works

Orca uses an automated market maker (AMM) model where liquidity providers deposit tokens into pools. Its Whirlpools feature allows LPs to concentrate their liquidity within specific price ranges, earning higher fees on their capital compared to traditional full-range pools. Traders benefit from tighter spreads and lower slippage.

### Why It Matters

Orca's focus on user experience and capital efficiency makes DeFi accessible to newcomers while offering sophisticated features for experienced traders. Its concentrated liquidity model helps LPs earn better returns, and its clean design sets a standard for how DeFi applications should feel on Solana.
