## Fren

**Fren** is a deliberate misspelling of "friend" commonly used in crypto and NFT communities. It conveys warmth and camaraderie between community members and is part of the distinctive informal language of Web3 culture.

### How It Works

"Fren" is used to address fellow community members in a friendly, informal way. Phrases like "gm frens" or "frens, check this out" are common across crypto social channels. Some NFT communities have adopted frog-themed characters associated with the term, building entire subcultures around the concept of being "frens."

### Why It Matters

The term reflects the community-first ethos of crypto culture, where relationships and belonging are valued alongside financial opportunity. It helps create an approachable atmosphere in otherwise technical and financially focused spaces.
