## Generative Art

**Generative art** refers to artwork created through algorithms, where code determines the visual output using randomized parameters. In the NFT space, generative art produces unique pieces at the moment of minting, making each token one-of-a-kind.

### How It Works

Artists write code that defines visual elements and their possible variations — colors, shapes, compositions, and patterns. When an NFT is minted, a random seed determines which combination of parameters is used, producing a unique artwork. The art lives on-chain or is generated from on-chain data, ensuring authenticity and provenance.

### Why It Matters

Generative art merges technology with creativity, allowing artists to create collections of thousands of unique works from a single algorithm. It has become one of the most respected categories in the NFT space, with platforms hosting generative art collections that push the boundaries of digital creativity.
