## NFT Marketplace

An **NFT marketplace** is a platform that facilitates the buying, selling, and trading of non-fungible tokens. Marketplaces provide interfaces for listing NFTs, browsing collections, placing bids, and completing transactions.

### How It Works

Sellers list their NFTs on the marketplace, setting a fixed price or starting an auction. Buyers browse listings, filter by collection or traits, and purchase using cryptocurrency. The marketplace smart contract handles escrow and settlement, and typically charges a small percentage fee. Some marketplaces also enforce creator royalties on secondary sales.

### Why It Matters

Marketplaces are the primary venue for NFT commerce and price discovery. On Solana, Magic Eden and Tensor are the leading marketplaces, offering advanced features like real-time analytics, trait-based trading, and compressed NFT support. The marketplace you choose affects fees, liquidity, and the types of NFTs available.
