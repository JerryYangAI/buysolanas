## Jito

**Jito** is a protocol on Solana focused on MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) and liquid staking. It provides a modified validator client that enables more efficient transaction ordering and offers JitoSOL, a liquid staking token similar to Marinade's mSOL but with additional MEV rewards passed on to stakers.

### How It Works

Jito's validator client allows searchers to submit transaction bundles — groups of transactions that must be executed in a specific order. This creates an organized MEV market rather than chaotic competition. When you stake SOL through Jito, you receive JitoSOL tokens and earn both standard staking rewards and a share of MEV tips that validators receive.

### Why It Matters

Jito has become a major force in the Solana ecosystem, with billions of dollars in staked SOL. By organizing MEV extraction into a transparent marketplace, Jito reduces the negative effects of MEV (like failed transactions and network spam) while sharing the profits with regular stakers. The JitoSOL token can be used across Solana DeFi just like any other token.
