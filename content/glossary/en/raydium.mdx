## Raydium

**Raydium** is one of the largest automated market maker (AMM) protocols on the Solana blockchain. It provides decentralized trading, liquidity provision, and yield farming opportunities. Raydium plays a central role in Solana's DeFi ecosystem as a primary source of on-chain liquidity.

### How It Works

Raydium uses liquidity pools where users deposit pairs of tokens. These pools enable other traders to swap between those tokens. In return, liquidity providers earn a share of the trading fees generated by the pool. Raydium also integrates with Solana's central order book, allowing its liquidity to be accessed by other protocols and aggregators.

### Why It Matters

Raydium is essential infrastructure for the Solana DeFi ecosystem. It provides the deep liquidity needed for efficient token trading and has been a launchpad for many new Solana tokens. If you are trading tokens on Solana through Jupiter or other aggregators, there is a good chance your trade is being routed through Raydium's liquidity pools.
