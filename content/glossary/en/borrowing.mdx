## Borrowing

**Borrowing** in DeFi means taking out a loan from a lending protocol by depositing crypto assets as collateral. Unlike traditional loans, DeFi borrowing requires no credit checks, paperwork, or approval process — it is instant and permissionless.

### How It Works

To borrow, you deposit collateral (e.g., SOL or ETH) into a lending protocol and can then borrow other tokens up to a percentage of your collateral value (the loan-to-value ratio). You pay interest on the borrowed amount, and if your collateral value drops too low relative to your loan, your position may be liquidated.

### Why It Matters

Borrowing enables users to access liquidity without selling their assets. This is useful for leverage trading, tax optimization, or accessing stablecoins for expenses while maintaining exposure to appreciating assets. It is a core primitive of decentralized finance.
