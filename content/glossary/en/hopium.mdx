## Hopium

**Hopium** is a blend of "hope" and "opium," describing unrealistic optimism about a crypto asset's future performance. Someone on hopium clings to overly bullish expectations despite contrary evidence.

### How It Works

Hopium manifests as persistently bullish sentiment regardless of market conditions — expecting a token to reach extreme price targets without fundamental justification, believing every dip is a buying opportunity, or assuming a troubled project will miraculously recover. It is closely related to copium but focuses on future expectations rather than rationalizing current losses.

### Why It Matters

Hopium can lead to poor investment decisions: holding through major downturns, averaging down into failing projects, or ignoring valid risk signals. Balanced analysis that considers both bullish and bearish scenarios leads to better outcomes than blind optimism.
