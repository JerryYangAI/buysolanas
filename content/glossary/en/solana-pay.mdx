## Solana Pay

**Solana Pay** is an open-source payment framework that allows merchants to accept payments directly on the Solana blockchain. It supports instant settlement in SOL, USDC, and other SPL tokens with near-zero transaction fees.

### How It Works

Merchants generate payment requests as QR codes or payment links. Customers scan the code with their Solana wallet and approve the transaction. The payment settles on-chain in seconds, with the merchant receiving tokens directly in their wallet. Solana Pay also supports transaction requests that can trigger complex on-chain actions like NFT minting upon payment.

### Why It Matters

Solana Pay offers a decentralized alternative to traditional payment processors, eliminating credit card fees and chargebacks. Its instant finality and minimal fees make it practical for everyday commerce, while its programmable nature enables innovative payment experiences like loyalty rewards and token-gated access.
