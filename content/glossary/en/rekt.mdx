## Rekt

**Rekt** is crypto slang derived from "wrecked," meaning to suffer severe financial losses. A trader who gets liquidated, scammed, or loses most of their portfolio is said to have gotten "rekt."

### How It Works

Getting rekt can happen through various means: a leveraged position being liquidated, buying into a rug pull, falling victim to a phishing attack, or simply buying the top and selling the bottom. The term is used both sympathetically and as cautionary tales within the community.

### Why It Matters

While often used humorously, getting rekt is a real risk in the volatile crypto market. The term serves as a reminder of the importance of risk management, proper research, and security practices. Learning from others who have been rekt — without experiencing it yourself — is one of the best ways to improve as a crypto participant.
