## Collection

A **collection** in the NFT space refers to a set of related non-fungible tokens that share a common theme, creator, smart contract, or artistic style. Collections can range from a few pieces to tens of thousands of items.

### How It Works

NFT collections are typically created under a single verified smart contract or collection address. On Solana, Metaplex provides collection verification that groups NFTs together. Each NFT in a collection has unique traits but shares the collection's overarching identity. Marketplaces organize and display NFTs by collection, showing floor prices and volume statistics.

### Why It Matters

Collections are the primary organizational unit for NFTs. They build brand identity, community, and liquidity around a shared set of assets. Collection-level metrics like floor price, volume, and holder count are key indicators used by traders and collectors to evaluate the market.
