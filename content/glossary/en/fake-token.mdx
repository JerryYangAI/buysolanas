## Fake Token

A **fake token** is a fraudulent cryptocurrency created to impersonate a legitimate project's token. Scammers copy the name, symbol, and sometimes the logo of a real token to trick users into buying the worthless counterfeit.

### How It Works

Anyone can create a token with any name on permissionless blockchains. Scammers create tokens mimicking popular projects and add liquidity on DEXs. Unsuspecting users searching for the real token may find and buy the fake version. Always verify the official contract address from the project's verified channels before trading.

### Why It Matters

Fake tokens are one of the most common scams in crypto. They exploit the permissionless nature of blockchain to deceive users. Always verify token contract addresses from official project sources, use verified token lists on DEXs, and be cautious of tokens that appear unexpectedly in your wallet.
