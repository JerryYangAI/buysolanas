## Proof of Stake (PoS)

**Proof of Stake** is a consensus mechanism where validators are selected to produce blocks based on the amount of cryptocurrency they have staked (locked up) as collateral. Solana uses a variant of PoS combined with Proof of History.

### How It Works

Validators lock up tokens as a stake, serving as a security deposit. The protocol selects validators to produce blocks, weighted by their stake size. Validators who behave honestly earn rewards, while those who act maliciously can have their stake slashed (partially confiscated). Delegators can stake their tokens with validators to earn a share of rewards.

### Why It Matters

PoS is dramatically more energy-efficient than Proof of Work and enables faster block times. It also allows token holders to participate in network security through staking. Solana's PoS system, combined with its other innovations, enables sub-second finality and thousands of transactions per second.
