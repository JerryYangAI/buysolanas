## Pyth Network

**Pyth Network** is a decentralized oracle solution originally built on Solana that provides high-fidelity, real-time price feeds for cryptocurrencies, equities, commodities, and forex. It sources data directly from first-party financial institutions and trading firms.

### How It Works

Unlike traditional oracles that scrape data from exchanges, Pyth receives price data directly from market participants like exchanges, market makers, and trading desks. These data providers publish prices on-chain with confidence intervals, and Pyth aggregates them into a single reliable price feed updated multiple times per second.

### Why It Matters

Accurate, low-latency price data is essential for DeFi protocols. Pyth's sub-second update frequency and first-party data sourcing make it ideal for applications like perpetual exchanges, lending protocols, and options platforms on Solana where stale prices could lead to incorrect liquidations or arbitrage exploits.
