## WAGMI

**WAGMI** stands for "We're All Gonna Make It." It is a popular expression in the cryptocurrency community used to express optimism, unity, and shared belief that the community will ultimately succeed and profit from their investments and involvement in crypto.

### Origin and Usage

WAGMI emerged from crypto Twitter and Discord communities as a rallying cry during both bull and bear markets. It is used to encourage fellow community members, celebrate positive developments, or maintain morale during difficult times. You will often see WAGMI in tweets, Discord messages, and comment sections when good news drops or when the community wants to show solidarity during a downturn.

### Why It Matters

WAGMI represents the communal spirit of crypto culture. Unlike traditional finance, crypto communities are highly collaborative, and this shared optimism helps onboard newcomers and maintain engagement through volatile periods. Its counterpart is NGMI ("Not Gonna Make It"), which is used to describe poor decisions or bearish behavior. Understanding these cultural expressions helps you navigate and connect with the Solana community and broader crypto ecosystem.
