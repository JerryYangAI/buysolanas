## Tokenomics

**Tokenomics** (token economics) encompasses the entire economic design of a cryptocurrency — how tokens are created, distributed, used, and valued. It includes supply mechanics, distribution schedules, utility within the ecosystem, and incentive structures.

### How It Works

Key tokenomics factors include: total and maximum supply, initial distribution (team, investors, community, treasury), vesting schedules, inflation/emission rates, token utility (governance, fees, staking), burn mechanisms, and revenue-sharing models. Well-designed tokenomics align incentives between all participants.

### Why It Matters

Tokenomics is arguably the most important factor in evaluating a crypto investment. Poor tokenomics — like excessive team allocations, short vesting periods, or high inflation — can doom even promising projects. Understanding tokenomics helps investors identify sustainable value versus short-term hype.
