## Token

A **token** is a digital asset that exists on a blockchain. Tokens can represent many things: cryptocurrency, governance voting rights, ownership shares, loyalty points, or any other unit of value. On Solana, tokens are created using the SPL (Solana Program Library) Token standard.

### Types of Tokens

**Fungible tokens** are interchangeable — one USDC is identical to any other USDC, just like one dollar bill is the same as another. **Non-fungible tokens (NFTs)** are unique — each one is different. **Governance tokens** give holders voting power in a protocol's decisions. **Stablecoins** are tokens pegged to real-world currencies like the US dollar. **Wrapped tokens** represent assets from other blockchains.

### Why It Matters

Tokens are the building blocks of the crypto economy. Everything you interact with on Solana — from trading on Jupiter to staking with Marinade to collecting NFTs on Magic Eden — involves tokens. Understanding token types helps you navigate the ecosystem. When evaluating a new token, consider its purpose, supply, distribution, and the team behind it. Always verify token addresses on Solscan to avoid fake or scam tokens.
