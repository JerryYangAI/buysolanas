## Wormhole

**Wormhole** is a leading cross-chain messaging and bridge protocol that connects Solana to Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, and over 20 other blockchains. It enables users to transfer tokens and data seamlessly between different networks.

### How It Works

Wormhole operates through a network of guardian nodes that observe transactions on one chain and attest to them, allowing equivalent actions on the destination chain. When you bridge tokens via Wormhole, the original tokens are locked on the source chain and wrapped versions are minted on the destination chain.

### Why It Matters

Wormhole is a critical infrastructure piece for Solana's ecosystem, allowing assets and liquidity to flow freely between blockchains. This interoperability expands the utility of Solana-based tokens and lets users access DeFi opportunities across multiple networks without being siloed in a single ecosystem.
