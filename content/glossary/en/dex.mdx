## DEX (Decentralized Exchange)

A **Decentralized Exchange (DEX)** is a cryptocurrency trading platform that operates without a central authority. Unlike centralized exchanges (like Binance or Coinbase) where the company holds your funds, a DEX lets you trade directly from your own wallet using smart contracts. You always maintain custody of your assets.

### How It Works

DEXs use smart contracts to facilitate trades automatically. Most DEXs on Solana use the AMM (Automated Market Maker) model, where trades are executed against liquidity pools rather than matched with other traders. When you swap tokens on a DEX, the smart contract handles the exchange instantly. Popular Solana DEXs include Raydium, Orca, and the Jupiter aggregator which routes trades across multiple DEXs for the best price.

### Why It Matters

DEXs are a cornerstone of the DeFi ecosystem because they provide permissionless trading — anyone can trade any available token at any time without creating an account or completing identity verification. On Solana, DEXs benefit from the chain's speed and low fees, making trading feel nearly instant. The tradeoff is that you are fully responsible for your own wallet security and cannot recover funds if you make a mistake.
