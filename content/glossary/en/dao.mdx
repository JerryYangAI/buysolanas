## DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)

A **DAO** is an organization governed by smart contracts and token-holder voting rather than a traditional management hierarchy. Members collectively make decisions about the organization's direction, finances, and operations through on-chain governance.

### How It Works

DAOs operate through proposals and votes. Any member (or member meeting a threshold) can submit a proposal, and token holders vote to approve or reject it. Approved proposals are executed automatically by smart contracts. DAOs manage shared treasuries and can control protocols, investment funds, NFT collections, and more.

### Why It Matters

DAOs represent a new model of organizational governance that is transparent, global, and permissionless. They enable communities to collectively own and operate protocols and projects without centralized leadership, aligning incentives across all participants.
