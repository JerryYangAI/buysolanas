## Reveal

A **reveal** is the event when newly minted NFTs transition from showing placeholder artwork to displaying their actual unique artwork and traits. Reveals build excitement and create a moment of discovery for minters.

### How It Works

During many NFT launches, all minted tokens initially show the same placeholder image. At a scheduled time after the mint completes, the project updates the metadata to reveal the actual artwork. This can happen all at once (instant reveal) or gradually. The reveal assigns random traits to each token, preventing minters from selectively minting only rare items.

### Why It Matters

Reveals create an engaging experience similar to opening a collectible pack. They ensure fair random distribution of rare traits, since no one knows which specific NFT they will receive during minting. The reveal moment often generates significant community excitement and trading activity.
