## Total Supply

**Total supply** is the total number of tokens that have been created and currently exist, including those that are locked, vesting, or otherwise not in public circulation. It does not include tokens that have been permanently burned.

### How It Works

Total supply = All minted tokens - Burned tokens. It includes circulating tokens plus those held in vesting contracts, team wallets, foundation reserves, and staking contracts. For tokens with no maximum supply cap, the total supply continues to grow through inflation or staking rewards.

### Why It Matters

Total supply helps investors understand the full picture of a token's economics. Combined with circulating supply, it shows how much future dilution to expect. A large gap between circulating and total supply indicates significant unreleased tokens that could impact price when they enter circulation.
