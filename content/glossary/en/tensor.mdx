## Tensor

**Tensor** is a professional NFT marketplace and trading platform built on Solana. It is designed for serious NFT traders who need advanced tools like real-time charts, collection-wide bidding, and detailed analytics — features traditionally found on stock trading platforms but applied to NFTs.

### Key Features

Tensor offers real-time price charts for NFT collections, the ability to place bids across entire collections or specific trait categories, portfolio tracking, and rarity-based filtering. Its AMM (Automated Market Maker) feature also allows users to create liquidity pools for NFT trading, enabling instant buys and sells.

### Why It Matters

While Magic Eden is known for its user-friendly approach, Tensor caters to power users and professional traders who need data-driven tools. The platform has grown to become one of the top NFT marketplaces on Solana by volume, and its TNSR token rewards active traders. Together, Tensor and Magic Eden form the backbone of Solana's NFT trading ecosystem.
