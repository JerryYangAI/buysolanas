## Degen

**Degen** (short for "degenerate") is a self-adopted label in the crypto community for traders who embrace extremely high-risk strategies. Degens typically chase new, unproven tokens, ape into projects without thorough research, and are comfortable with the possibility of losing everything for the chance of massive gains.

### Usage and Context

The term "degen" is used both as self-identification and to describe others. Someone might say "I'm being a total degen today" after buying a brand new memecoin, or "degen plays only" when referring to high-risk trading strategies. On Solana, degen culture is especially prominent due to the chain's low fees making it easy to trade many small, speculative tokens frequently.

### Why It Matters

While degen culture adds excitement to the crypto ecosystem, it carries real financial risk. Many degen trades result in significant losses, especially in new tokens that may be scams or rug pulls. If you find yourself drawn to degen behavior, practice responsible risk management: never invest more than you can afford to lose, always keep the majority of your portfolio in established assets, and treat degen trading as entertainment rather than a reliable investment strategy.
