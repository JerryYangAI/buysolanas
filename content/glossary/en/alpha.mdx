## Alpha

**Alpha** in crypto refers to exclusive or early-stage information that gives a trader or investor an informational advantage over the broader market. Finding "alpha" means discovering opportunities before they become widely known.

### How It Works

Alpha can come from research, community connections, on-chain analysis, or insider knowledge. Examples include discovering a promising project before its token launch, identifying an upcoming partnership, or spotting a DeFi yield opportunity before it becomes crowded. Crypto communities often share alpha in private groups or paid channels.

### Why It Matters

In crypto's competitive landscape, having informational edges can lead to outsized returns. However, acting on true insider information may have legal implications, and claimed "alpha" from social media should always be verified with personal research. Not all alpha is reliable, and following unverified tips can lead to losses.
