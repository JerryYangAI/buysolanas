## Trait

A **trait** is an individual attribute or characteristic of an NFT within a generative collection. Traits define what makes each NFT unique, including elements like background color, hairstyle, clothing, accessories, and expressions.

### How It Works

Generative NFT collections define categories of traits (e.g., "Background," "Eyes," "Hat") with multiple possible values in each category. During minting, traits are randomly assigned based on predefined probabilities. Some traits are common (appearing in 50%+ of the collection) while others are extremely rare (appearing in less than 1%).

### Why It Matters

Traits are the building blocks of NFT identity and value. Specific traits can become highly desirable within a community, driving demand for NFTs that possess them. Trait-based filtering on marketplaces allows collectors to search for specific combinations, and trait rarity directly influences pricing.
