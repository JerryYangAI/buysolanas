## Drainer

A **drainer** is a malicious smart contract or script designed to steal tokens and NFTs from a victim's wallet. Drainers are typically deployed through phishing sites that trick users into signing seemingly harmless transactions that actually grant full wallet access.

### How It Works

Victims are lured to fake websites mimicking popular projects or airdrops. The site prompts a wallet connection and presents a transaction to sign. This transaction contains hidden approvals or transfer instructions that allow the drainer contract to withdraw all valuable tokens and NFTs from the wallet in a single transaction.

### Why It Matters

Drainers have become increasingly sophisticated and are responsible for significant losses in the crypto space. Always verify the URL of any site you connect your wallet to, use transaction simulation features in your wallet to preview what a transaction will do, and never sign transactions you do not fully understand.
