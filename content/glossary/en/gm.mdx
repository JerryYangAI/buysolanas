## GM

**GM** stands for "Good Morning" and is a ubiquitous greeting in the crypto community. It is used across Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram as a way to acknowledge fellow community members and spread positive vibes.

### How It Works

Crypto community members post "GM" (or "gm") as a daily ritual, often first thing in the morning. Others respond with "GM" back, creating a sense of community and camaraderie. Some NFT projects and DAOs have built cultures around the GM greeting, and it has become a symbol of crypto culture.

### Why It Matters

While seemingly trivial, GM culture reflects the community-driven nature of crypto. It fosters connection between anonymous participants across the globe and signals engagement in the broader Web3 community. Responding to GMs is considered good etiquette in most crypto social spaces.
