## Edition

An **edition** in NFT terms refers to one copy of a multi-copy NFT artwork. Unlike 1/1 unique NFTs, editions are multiple tokens that share the same artwork but each have a unique edition number (e.g., #42 of 500).

### How It Works

Edition NFTs are minted from a master edition, producing numbered copies. Limited editions have a fixed maximum supply set at creation (e.g., 100 copies), while open editions have unlimited supply during a time window. Each edition token is a distinct NFT with its own on-chain identity, but they share the same visual content.

### Why It Matters

Editions make art more accessible by allowing multiple collectors to own the same piece at a lower price point than a unique 1/1. They bridge the gap between mass-produced items and unique artworks, letting artists reach larger audiences while collectors can own verified copies of works they appreciate.
