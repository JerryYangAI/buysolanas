## Stop-Loss

A **stop-loss** is a risk management order that automatically triggers a sell when an asset's price falls to a predetermined level. It limits the maximum loss a trader is willing to accept on a position.

### How It Works

Traders set a stop-loss price below their entry price. If the market price reaches this level, the stop-loss triggers and the position is sold at the best available price. On Solana DEXs, stop-losses can be implemented through limit orders on platforms like Jupiter, where keeper bots monitor prices and execute the sale when conditions are met.

### Why It Matters

Stop-losses are essential risk management tools that protect traders from large losses, especially in the volatile crypto market. They allow traders to define their maximum acceptable loss in advance and walk away from their screens without worrying about sudden price drops destroying their portfolio.
