## LFG

**LFG** stands for "Let's F***ing Go" and is an expression of extreme enthusiasm and excitement used throughout crypto culture. It is typically used when something positive happens — a token pumps, a project launches, or good news breaks.

### How It Works

LFG is used as a rallying cry in Discord servers, Twitter posts, and Telegram groups. It signals bullish excitement and community momentum. The phrase is often accompanied by rocket emojis and used during token launches, NFT mints, protocol milestones, and market rallies.

### Why It Matters

LFG encapsulates the high-energy, optimistic culture of crypto communities. While it reflects genuine enthusiasm, it can also signal hype-driven behavior. New users should balance community excitement with their own research before making investment decisions based on crowd sentiment.
