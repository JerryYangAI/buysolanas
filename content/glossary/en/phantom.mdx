## Phantom

**Phantom** is the leading cryptocurrency wallet built for the Solana ecosystem. Available as a browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave) and mobile app (iOS, Android), Phantom allows users to store, send, receive, and swap tokens, as well as interact with decentralized applications (dApps) on Solana.

### Key Features

Phantom offers a clean, user-friendly interface for managing your crypto assets. You can view your token balances and NFT collection, swap tokens directly within the wallet, stake SOL to earn rewards, and connect to thousands of Solana dApps. It also supports multiple blockchains including Ethereum and Polygon.

### Why It Matters

A wallet is your gateway to the blockchain world, and Phantom has become the go-to choice for Solana users due to its simplicity and reliability. For newcomers to Solana, setting up a Phantom wallet is typically the first step. It generates a seed phrase that you must keep safe, as it is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.
