## Proof of History (PoH)

**Proof of History** is a novel consensus innovation developed by Solana founder Anatoly Yakovenko. It acts as a cryptographic clock that creates a verifiable sequence of events over time, allowing the network to agree on the order and timing of transactions without validators needing to communicate with each other constantly.

### How It Works

PoH uses a sequential hashing function (SHA-256) that runs continuously, producing a verifiable chain of outputs. Each output depends on the previous one, creating an unbreakable timeline. When a transaction is inserted into this sequence, everyone can verify exactly when it happened relative to other events.

### Why It Matters

Traditional blockchains like Bitcoin and Ethereum spend significant time getting validators to agree on what happened and when. PoH eliminates this bottleneck by pre-ordering transactions before consensus begins. This is a key reason why Solana can process thousands of transactions per second while maintaining security and decentralization.
