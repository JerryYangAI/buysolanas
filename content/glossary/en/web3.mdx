## Web3

**Web3** represents the next evolution of the internet, built on decentralized technologies like blockchain. Unlike Web2 (centralized platforms like Google and Facebook), Web3 aims to give users ownership of their data, digital identity, and assets without relying on intermediaries.

### How It Works

Web3 applications run on decentralized networks rather than centralized servers. Users interact through crypto wallets instead of traditional accounts, own their digital assets as tokens and NFTs, and participate in governance through DAOs. Data is stored on blockchains and decentralized storage rather than corporate databases.

### Why It Matters

Web3 represents a fundamental shift in how the internet works — from platform-controlled to user-owned. It enables new models for finance (DeFi), identity (self-sovereign), content ownership (NFTs), and organizational governance (DAOs). Solana is a leading blockchain for building fast, affordable Web3 applications.
