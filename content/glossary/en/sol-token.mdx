## SOL Token

**SOL** is the native cryptocurrency of the Solana blockchain. Like ETH on Ethereum or BTC on Bitcoin, SOL is the fundamental currency that powers the entire Solana ecosystem. It is used to pay transaction fees, participate in staking, and govern the network's future direction.

### Key Uses

SOL serves three primary purposes: paying for transaction fees (which are typically less than $0.01), staking to help secure the network and earn rewards, and participating in governance decisions about protocol upgrades. SOL can be divided into smaller units called "lamports," where 1 SOL equals 1 billion lamports.

### Why It Matters

As the native token, SOL's value is closely tied to the health and adoption of the Solana ecosystem. When more people use Solana-based applications, demand for SOL increases because it is needed for every transaction. SOL can be purchased on major cryptocurrency exchanges and stored in wallets like Phantom.
