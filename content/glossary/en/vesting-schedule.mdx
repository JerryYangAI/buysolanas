## Vesting Schedule

A **vesting schedule** defines the specific timeline and rules for how allocated tokens are gradually released to their recipients. It specifies the total amount, duration, cliff period, and release frequency.

### How It Works

A typical vesting schedule might read: "4-year vest with 1-year cliff, monthly linear unlock." This means no tokens for the first year, then equal monthly portions over the remaining 3 years. Vesting can be linear (steady releases), stepped (quarterly chunks), or milestone-based (released upon achieving specific goals).

### Why It Matters

Vesting schedules directly impact token supply and price dynamics. Large upcoming unlocks can create selling pressure. Investors should always check a project's vesting schedule to understand when significant token supply increases will occur and how they might affect the market.
