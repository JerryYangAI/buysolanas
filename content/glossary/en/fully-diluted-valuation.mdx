## Fully Diluted Valuation (FDV)

**Fully diluted valuation** is the theoretical total market capitalization of a cryptocurrency if the maximum token supply were all in circulation at the current price. It represents the project's total potential valuation.

### How It Works

FDV = Current Price x Maximum (or Total) Supply. For example, if a token trades at $10 with 10 million tokens circulating but a max supply of 100 million, the market cap is $100 million but the FDV is $1 billion. The gap between market cap and FDV indicates future dilution potential.

### Why It Matters

FDV reveals the true cost of a project at current prices. A low market cap with a very high FDV suggests significant future token unlocks that could create selling pressure. Comparing FDV to market cap helps investors understand how much dilution is coming and whether the current price adequately accounts for future supply increases.
