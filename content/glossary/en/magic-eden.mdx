## Magic Eden

**Magic Eden** is the largest and most popular NFT marketplace originally built on Solana, now expanded to support multiple blockchains including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Polygon. It provides an accessible platform for users to buy, sell, discover, and create NFTs.

### Key Features

Magic Eden offers a clean browsing experience for exploring NFT collections, tools for creators to launch their own collections (Launchpad), and a straightforward listing and purchasing process. It supports auction-style sales, fixed-price listings, and offers-based trading. The platform also features curated collections and trending data to help users discover new projects.

### Why It Matters

Magic Eden played a crucial role in growing Solana's NFT ecosystem from its early days. Its user-friendly interface lowered the barrier to entry for NFT trading on Solana, attracting both creators and collectors. By expanding to other blockchains, Magic Eden has become a cross-chain NFT hub, but Solana remains a core part of its identity and trading volume.
