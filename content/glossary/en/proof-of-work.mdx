## Proof of Work (PoW)

**Proof of Work** is the original blockchain consensus mechanism, used by Bitcoin. Miners compete to solve complex mathematical puzzles, and the winner gets to add the next block of transactions to the chain and receive a reward.

### How It Works

Miners use computational power to find a hash value that meets specific criteria (a target difficulty). This requires enormous amounts of trial-and-error computation. The first miner to find a valid hash broadcasts the block, and other nodes verify it. The difficulty adjusts to maintain consistent block times.

### Why It Matters

PoW provides strong security through the economic cost of computation — attacking the network would require more computing power than all honest miners combined. However, its high energy consumption and slow throughput led newer blockchains like Solana to adopt Proof of Stake as a more efficient alternative.
