## Seed Phrase

A **seed phrase** (also called a recovery phrase or mnemonic phrase) is a sequence of 12 or 24 randomly generated words that serves as the master key to your cryptocurrency wallet. It can be used to recover your wallet and all its funds on any compatible device, making it the most critical piece of information to protect.

### How It Works

When you create a new wallet (like Phantom), the software generates a random seed phrase and derives all your private keys and wallet addresses from it. This means that anyone who knows your seed phrase can recreate your entire wallet and access all your funds. The words come from a standardized list (BIP-39) and must be kept in the exact order they were given.

### Why It Matters

Your seed phrase is the ultimate backup for your crypto assets. If your phone breaks, your computer crashes, or you lose access to your wallet app, your seed phrase is the only way to recover your funds. Never share your seed phrase with anyone, never store it digitally (screenshots, emails, or cloud storage), and never enter it on any website. Write it down on paper and store it in a secure physical location. No legitimate service will ever ask for your seed phrase.
