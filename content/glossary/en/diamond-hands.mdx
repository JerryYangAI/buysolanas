## Diamond Hands

**Diamond hands** is a crypto and trading expression that describes someone who holds onto their investments with unwavering conviction, refusing to sell even during sharp price drops or extreme market volatility. The metaphor suggests hands so strong and unbreakable (like diamonds) that nothing can make them let go of their position.

### Usage and Context

The term is often represented with diamond and hand emojis in social media posts. Someone with diamond hands is praised for their mental fortitude during bear markets or flash crashes. The opposite is "paper hands" — someone who sells at the first sign of trouble. In the Solana community, diamond hands might refer to long-term SOL holders who held through market downturns and were rewarded when prices recovered.

### Why It Matters

Diamond hands embodies the psychological challenge of investing: the ability to withstand short-term pain for potential long-term gain. While holding through volatility has historically been rewarded for strong projects, blind diamond-handing can also lead to losses if the fundamental value of an asset has genuinely declined. The healthiest approach is informed diamond hands — holding based on solid research and a clear thesis, not just stubbornness or community pressure.
