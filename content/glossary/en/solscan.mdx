## Solscan

**Solscan** is the most widely used blockchain explorer for the Solana network. A blockchain explorer is like a search engine for the blockchain — it allows anyone to look up transactions, wallet addresses, token information, and overall network statistics in a user-friendly interface.

### Key Features

Solscan lets you search for any transaction by its signature to see its status, fees, and involved accounts. You can view any wallet's token holdings, transaction history, and NFT collection. It also provides network-wide analytics including TPS (transactions per second), validator information, and token market data.

### Why It Matters

Transparency is a core principle of blockchain technology, and Solscan makes that transparency accessible to everyone. Whether you want to verify that a payment was received, check the details of a token before buying, or research a project's on-chain activity, Solscan is the go-to tool. It is often the first place people check when they want to confirm a Solana transaction.
