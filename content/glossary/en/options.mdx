## Options

**Options** are financial derivatives that give the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy (call option) or sell (put option) an asset at a predetermined price (strike price) before or on a specific date.

### How It Works

A call option profits when the asset price rises above the strike price, while a put option profits when it falls below. Option buyers pay a premium for this right. In DeFi, options protocols use smart contracts to create, settle, and exercise options without intermediaries. Vaults often sell options automatically to generate yield.

### Why It Matters

Options provide sophisticated risk management and income generation strategies. They allow traders to hedge positions, speculate with limited downside, and earn premiums. DeFi options protocols are bringing these tools to crypto in a permissionless and transparent way.
