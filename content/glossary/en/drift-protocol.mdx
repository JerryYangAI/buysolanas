## Drift Protocol

**Drift Protocol** is a decentralized perpetual futures exchange built on Solana. It allows traders to go long or short on various crypto assets with leverage, and features a dynamic AMM combined with an on-chain order book for deep liquidity.

### How It Works

Drift uses a hybrid model combining a virtual AMM with just-in-time (JIT) liquidity from market makers and a decentralized limit order book. This approach provides better pricing than pure AMM models. Traders can open leveraged positions, and the protocol uses an insurance fund and backstop liquidity to manage risk.

### Why It Matters

Drift brings sophisticated derivatives trading to Solana in a fully decentralized manner. Its hybrid liquidity model reduces slippage for traders while offering LPs multiple ways to earn yield, making perpetual futures accessible without relying on centralized exchanges.
