## Fundamental Analysis

**Fundamental analysis** (FA) evaluates a crypto project's intrinsic value by examining factors beyond price charts — including technology, team, tokenomics, adoption metrics, revenue, competitive landscape, and market opportunity.

### How It Works

Fundamental analysts research a project's whitepaper, code quality, developer activity, on-chain metrics (TVL, active users, transaction volume), token distribution, revenue model, and competitive advantages. They assess whether the current market price reflects the project's true value, identifying overvalued or undervalued tokens.

### Why It Matters

While technical analysis focuses on price patterns, fundamental analysis helps investors understand what they are actually buying. Strong fundamentals suggest long-term viability regardless of short-term price fluctuations. For Solana ecosystem tokens, evaluating protocol revenue, user growth, and developer activity provides crucial insight into sustainable value.
