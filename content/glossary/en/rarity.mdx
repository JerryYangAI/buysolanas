## Rarity

**Rarity** in NFTs refers to how uncommon or unique a particular NFT is within its collection based on its combination of traits. Rarer NFTs typically command higher prices in the market.

### How It Works

Each NFT in a generative collection has a combination of traits (background, clothing, accessories, etc.). Rarity is calculated based on how frequently each trait appears across the entire collection. An NFT with multiple rare traits scores higher in rarity rankings. Tools and marketplaces display rarity scores and rankings to help buyers evaluate items.

### Why It Matters

Rarity is one of the primary value drivers in NFT collections. Collectors seek rare pieces for their uniqueness and investment potential. However, rarity alone does not determine value — aesthetic appeal, cultural significance, and community perception also play important roles in pricing.
