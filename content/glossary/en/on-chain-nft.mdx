## On-Chain NFT

An **on-chain NFT** is a non-fungible token whose artwork, metadata, and all associated data are stored directly on the blockchain rather than on external storage systems like IPFS or centralized servers.

### How It Works

Most NFTs store only a pointer (URL) on-chain, with the actual image hosted elsewhere. On-chain NFTs encode the entire artwork — often as SVG code or pixel data — directly in the blockchain's storage. This means the NFT exists entirely within the blockchain and does not depend on any external service to display correctly.

### Why It Matters

On-chain NFTs offer the strongest permanence guarantee. External storage can go offline, IPFS pins can be lost, and servers can shut down, potentially leaving NFTs as broken links. Fully on-chain NFTs will exist as long as the blockchain itself operates, making them truly permanent digital artifacts.
