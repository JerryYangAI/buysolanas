## marginfi

**marginfi** is a leading decentralized lending and borrowing protocol on Solana. Users can deposit crypto assets to earn interest or borrow against their collateral, with the protocol managing risk through dynamic interest rates and liquidation mechanisms.

### How It Works

Lenders deposit supported tokens into marginfi's pools and receive interest generated from borrowers. Borrowers post collateral and can take loans up to a certain loan-to-value ratio. If a borrower's collateral value drops below the required threshold, their position is liquidated to protect lenders.

### Why It Matters

marginfi provides essential lending infrastructure for Solana's DeFi ecosystem. It enables capital efficiency by allowing users to put idle assets to work earning yield, while borrowers can access liquidity without selling their holdings.
