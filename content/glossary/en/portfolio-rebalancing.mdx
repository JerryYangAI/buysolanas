## Portfolio Rebalancing

**Portfolio rebalancing** is the process of periodically adjusting the allocation of assets in your crypto portfolio to maintain your target percentages. As token prices change, your portfolio naturally drifts from your intended allocation.

### How It Works

You set target allocations (e.g., 50% SOL, 30% stablecoins, 20% altcoins). Over time, outperforming assets grow to a larger percentage of your portfolio. Rebalancing involves selling some of the overweight assets and buying underweight ones to restore target allocations. This can be done manually or through automated DeFi tools.

### Why It Matters

Rebalancing enforces disciplined investing by systematically selling high and buying low. It manages risk by preventing over-concentration in any single asset. Regular rebalancing has been shown to improve risk-adjusted returns in traditional and crypto portfolios alike.
