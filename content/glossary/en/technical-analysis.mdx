## Technical Analysis

**Technical analysis** (TA) is a method of evaluating crypto assets by studying price charts, trading patterns, technical indicators, and volume data to identify trends and predict future price movements.

### How It Works

Technical analysts use tools like moving averages, RSI (Relative Strength Index), MACD, Bollinger Bands, and support/resistance levels to interpret price action. Chart patterns like head-and-shoulders, triangles, and flags signal potential price movements. TA is based on the premise that historical price patterns tend to repeat and that all known information is already reflected in the price.

### Why It Matters

Technical analysis is widely used by crypto traders to time entries and exits. While no method perfectly predicts the future, TA provides a framework for making informed trading decisions. Combined with fundamental analysis and proper risk management, it is an essential skill for active traders in the Solana ecosystem and beyond.
