## Sanctum

**Sanctum** is a Solana-native protocol that solves the liquidity fragmentation problem in liquid staking. It provides a unified liquidity layer that allows users to instantly swap between different liquid staking tokens (LSTs) and unstake SOL without waiting for the standard cooldown period.

### How It Works

Sanctum maintains a reserve pool of SOL that facilitates instant unstaking from any supported LST. It also operates an infinity pool that acts as a multi-LST AMM, enabling seamless swaps between different LSTs with minimal slippage. This unified approach means LST providers do not each need their own deep liquidity pools.

### Why It Matters

By unifying LST liquidity, Sanctum makes liquid staking more accessible and capital efficient on Solana. Users can freely move between different staking providers, and new LSTs can launch without needing to bootstrap their own liquidity from scratch.
