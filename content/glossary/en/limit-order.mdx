## Limit Order

A **limit order** is a type of trade order that specifies the exact price at which you want to buy or sell a token. Unlike market orders that execute immediately at the current price, limit orders wait until the market price reaches your specified level.

### How It Works

On Solana, platforms like Jupiter offer on-chain limit orders. You set your desired price and the order is placed on-chain. When the market price reaches your target, keeper bots detect the opportunity and execute the trade on your behalf using the best available liquidity across Solana DEXs.

### Why It Matters

Limit orders give traders precise control over their entry and exit prices. They are essential for executing trading strategies that require specific price levels and help avoid the slippage that comes with market orders in volatile conditions.
