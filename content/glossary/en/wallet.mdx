## Wallet

A **cryptocurrency wallet** is a tool that stores your private keys and allows you to send, receive, and manage your digital assets on the blockchain. Despite the name, a wallet does not actually "store" your crypto — your assets live on the blockchain. The wallet stores the keys that prove you own them.

### Types of Wallets

There are several types of wallets. **Hot wallets** (like Phantom, Solflare) are software applications connected to the internet, offering convenience for daily use. **Cold wallets** (like Ledger, Trezor) are hardware devices that store keys offline, providing maximum security for long-term holdings. **Browser extension wallets** let you connect directly to dApps. **Mobile wallets** offer on-the-go access.

### Why It Matters

Your wallet is the gateway to everything in crypto — from trading and DeFi to NFTs and dApps. Choosing the right wallet depends on your needs: hot wallets for convenience and frequent trading, cold wallets for securing large holdings. Most Solana users start with Phantom, which offers a balance of ease of use and features. Regardless of which wallet you use, securing your seed phrase is the most important step.
