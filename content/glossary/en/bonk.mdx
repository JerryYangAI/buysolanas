## BONK

**BONK** is a Solana-native meme coin featuring a Shiba Inu dog mascot. Launched in December 2022, it was airdropped to active Solana community members, NFT holders, developers, and DeFi users as a way to re-energize the ecosystem during a bear market.

### How It Works

BONK operates as an SPL token on Solana with a large total supply. It was distributed broadly through airdrops rather than a private sale, emphasizing community ownership. BONK has been integrated into numerous Solana dApps and DEXs, and features token burn mechanisms to gradually reduce supply.

### Why It Matters

BONK played a significant cultural role in reviving Solana community spirit during the bear market. Beyond its meme status, it demonstrated the power of community-driven token distribution and became one of the most widely held tokens in the Solana ecosystem.
