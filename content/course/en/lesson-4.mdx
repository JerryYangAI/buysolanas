## How to Buy SOL

You have your Phantom wallet set up. Now it is time to get some SOL into it. There are several ways to buy SOL, and this lesson walks you through the most common and safest methods.

### Method 1: Buy Through a Centralized Exchange

The most popular way to buy SOL is through a **centralized exchange (CEX)**. These are platforms that let you convert regular money (USD, EUR, etc.) into crypto.

Popular exchanges that support SOL:

- **Coinbase** — Beginner-friendly, available in many countries
- **Binance** — Largest by volume, lower fees
- **Kraken** — Strong security reputation
- **OKX** — Popular in Asia, feature-rich

**How it works:**

1. **Create an account** on the exchange and complete identity verification (KYC).
2. **Deposit funds** using a bank transfer, debit card, or other payment method.
3. **Search for SOL** and place a buy order.
4. **Withdraw to your Phantom wallet** — Copy your Phantom wallet address and use the exchange's withdrawal feature. Select the **Solana network** (not ERC-20 or BEP-20).

### Method 2: Buy Directly in Phantom

Phantom has a built-in feature to buy SOL with a credit card or Apple Pay through partners like MoonPay and Coinbase Pay. This is the simplest method but may have slightly higher fees.

1. Open Phantom and tap the **"Buy"** button.
2. Select your payment method and amount.
3. Complete the purchase — SOL arrives directly in your wallet.

### Method 3: Peer-to-Peer

Some users buy SOL directly from other people through peer-to-peer platforms. This method is common in regions with limited exchange access but carries higher risk. Only use reputable platforms with escrow protection.

### Important Safety Tips

- **Double-check the network** — When withdrawing from an exchange, always select the Solana (SOL) network. Sending SOL to an Ethereum address will result in lost funds.
- **Start small** — Make your first transfer a small test amount (e.g., 0.1 SOL) to confirm everything works before sending larger amounts.
- **Verify your wallet address** — Copy-paste your address carefully. Some malware can swap clipboard addresses.
- **Keep records** — Note your purchase price and dates for tax purposes.

### Understanding Transaction Fees

| Method | Typical Fee |
|--------|------------|
| Exchange purchase | 0.1% – 1.5% |
| Bank transfer deposit | Free – $5 |
| Card purchase in Phantom | 2% – 5% |
| Withdrawal to wallet | ~0.01 SOL |

### After Your Purchase

Once SOL arrives in your Phantom wallet, you will see your balance update. Your first transaction might take a few minutes if you are withdrawing from an exchange. After that, Solana transactions typically confirm in under a second.

You now own SOL and have full control of it in your own wallet. In the next lesson, we will explore what you can do with it.

### Key Takeaway

The safest way to buy SOL is through a reputable exchange, then transfer it to your own Phantom wallet. Always verify the network and wallet address before sending, and start with a small test transaction.
