## Create Your First Phantom Wallet

Before you can buy, send, or use SOL, you need a **wallet**. Think of it as your personal bank account on the blockchain — except you are the only one who controls it. The most popular Solana wallet is **Phantom**, and setting it up takes about 3 minutes.

### What Is a Crypto Wallet?

A crypto wallet does not actually "hold" your coins. Your assets live on the blockchain. The wallet holds your **private keys** — the passwords that prove you own those assets. Whoever has the keys controls the funds.

There are two types of wallets:

- **Hot wallets** — Connected to the internet (browser extensions, mobile apps). Convenient for daily use.
- **Cold wallets** — Offline devices (like Ledger). Best for storing large amounts securely.

Phantom is a hot wallet available as a **browser extension** and a **mobile app**.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Step 1: Download Phantom**

Visit [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) and install the extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge) or download the mobile app (iOS or Android). Always download from the official site to avoid fake versions.

**Step 2: Create a New Wallet**

Open Phantom and select "Create a new wallet." You will be asked to set a password. This password unlocks Phantom on your device but is not your main backup — that comes next.

**Step 3: Save Your Secret Recovery Phrase**

Phantom will show you a **12-word recovery phrase** (also called a seed phrase). This is the most important step.

> **Write these 12 words down on paper.** Store them in a safe place. Never share them with anyone. Never save them in a screenshot or text file on your computer.

If you lose access to your device, this phrase is the only way to recover your wallet. If someone else gets your phrase, they can steal everything in your wallet.

**Step 4: Confirm Your Phrase**

Phantom will ask you to verify some of the words. Complete this step to finish setup.

### Security Best Practices

- **Never share your seed phrase** — No legitimate service will ever ask for it.
- **Beware of phishing sites** — Always check the URL before entering any information.
- **Use a strong password** — Make your Phantom password unique and complex.
- **Consider a hardware wallet** — For amounts you cannot afford to lose, pair Phantom with a Ledger device.
- **Bookmark the official site** — Access Phantom only through your bookmark, not through search results.

### Your Wallet Address

After setup, you will see your wallet address — a long string like `7xKX...a3Fp`. This is safe to share publicly. People use it to send you SOL, just like an email address for crypto.

### Key Takeaway

Your Phantom wallet is your gateway to Solana. The seed phrase is your ultimate backup and must be kept offline and private. With your wallet ready, you are prepared to receive and use SOL.
