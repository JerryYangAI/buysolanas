## Solana NFTs: Tensor & Magic Eden

Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on Solana are digital assets that represent unique items — art, collectibles, gaming assets, and more. Solana's low fees make it ideal for NFT trading, where Ethereum gas costs can eat into profits on smaller transactions. Two marketplaces dominate the Solana NFT space: Tensor and Magic Eden.

### Tensor: The Pro Trading Platform

Tensor is designed for active NFT traders who want speed and advanced tools. Key features include:

- **Real-time floor price tracking** — see live price changes across collections.
- **Collection bids** — place a bid on any NFT in a collection at a price you set.
- **AMM pools** — automated market maker functionality for NFTs, similar to DeFi token pools.
- **TensorSwap** — instantly buy or sell NFTs at market prices.
- **Portfolio analytics** — track your NFT holdings, profit/loss, and historical trades.

Tensor rewards active traders with TNSR tokens through its points program, making it attractive for high-volume users.

### Magic Eden: The All-in-One Marketplace

Magic Eden is the most well-known NFT marketplace on Solana and supports multiple chains. It offers:

- **User-friendly interface** — great for beginners discovering collections.
- **Launchpad** — new NFT collections often mint directly through Magic Eden.
- **Cross-chain support** — browse Ethereum, Polygon, and Bitcoin NFTs alongside Solana.
- **Rarity tools** — check how rare specific traits are within a collection.

### How to Buy an NFT

1. Connect your wallet to Tensor or Magic Eden.
2. Browse collections or search for a specific project.
3. Check the **floor price** — the lowest listed price in the collection.
4. Review the NFT's traits and rarity ranking.
5. Click "Buy" and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

The entire process takes seconds and costs fractions of a cent in network fees.

### How to Sell an NFT

1. Navigate to your profile on the marketplace.
2. Select the NFT you want to sell.
3. Choose "List" and set your asking price.
4. Alternatively, accept an existing collection bid for instant sale.

### Evaluating a Collection

Before buying, research the project:

- **Floor price trend** — is the floor rising, stable, or declining?
- **Volume** — higher 24-hour trading volume indicates active interest.
- **Unique holders** — more unique holders suggest broader distribution and less whale manipulation.
- **Team and roadmap** — does the team have a credible track record?
- **Community** — check Discord and Twitter activity for genuine engagement.

### Marketplace Fees

| Marketplace | Buyer Fee | Seller Fee | Creator Royalties |
|-------------|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| Tensor | 0% | 1.5% | Optional |
| Magic Eden | 0% | 2% | Optional |

Creator royalties are payments to the original artist on secondary sales. Some marketplaces allow buyers to skip royalties, though supporting creators is encouraged.

### Safety When Trading NFTs

- **Verify collection links** — always navigate to marketplaces directly, never click links from DMs.
- **Check verified badges** — both Tensor and Magic Eden verify legitimate collections.
- **Beware of wash trading** — artificially inflated volume where the same person buys and sells to themselves.
- **Never share your seed phrase** — no legitimate platform or person will ask for it.
